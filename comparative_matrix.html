04

Cross-case takeaways

Possible commonality

In both cases, the role problem may be less about "impact" and more about how to name artistic research as part of collaborative knowledge-making.

Possible divergence

HLaS may reveal the institutional and evaluative side more sharply, while ATMOS_FERA may reveal the epistemic and experiential side more sharply.

05

What would count as enough

  • Not "proof" that EMI works universally.
  • Two reflective, well-structured case analyses showing why the need for EMI emerges.
  • Best evidence: concrete traces of role ambiguity, reframing, friction, or experiential shift.
  • Best conclusion: EMI is worth testing further, not already validated.
